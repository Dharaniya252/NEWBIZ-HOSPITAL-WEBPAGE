HISTORY

Founded in 2010, Newbiz Hospital was born out of a vision to revolutionize healthcare in our community. The hospital's inception was driven by a dedicated team of healthcare professionals and community leaders who recognized the need for accessible, quality medical care.

In the early days, Newbiz began as a modest clinic with a handful of beds and a small staff, focused on providing exceptional patient care. Our founders believed that every individual deserves personalized attention and compassionate treatment, regardless of their background.

SERVICES

Pharmacy Services

Emergency and Trauma Care
